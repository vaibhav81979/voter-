Voter Information
Eligibility to Vote
Must be a citizen of India
Must be 18 years of age or above
Must have a valid Voter ID (EPIC Number)
Must be registered in the electoral roll
Device with camera and fingerprint sensor required
What You Need
Your Voter ID / EPIC Number
Aadhaar-linked mobile number (for OTP)
Device with working front camera
Fingerprint sensor on your device
Stable internet connection
