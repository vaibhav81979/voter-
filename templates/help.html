Frequently Asked Questions

help How do I login to vote?

Go to Voter Login, enter your EPIC ID (Voter ID) and Aadhaar number. A camera prompt will appear for facial and biometric verification before proceeding.

help What if my camera fails?

Make sure your browser has permission to access the camera. If issues persist, test the camera in a different browser or visit your nearest designated polling station.

help How is my vote kept secret?

Votes are end-to-end encrypted and completely anonymized. Even administrators cannot trace a vote back to the voter who cast it.
